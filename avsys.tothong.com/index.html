sdub the app

By: firm
15-05-2012
final project, homework

Sdub is an augmented sound app that exploring a new interaction mobile device provide with sound. This new medium let us redefine our relationship with recorded sound and sonic environment. It offered a new way to listen and to play with sound. The app focuses on how sound can be a very powerful medium to evoke our awareness. Inspired by Eames’ The Power of Ten I seek to take that awareness of scale and sonify it into this app.

some more research side of this project can be found at:
interactivity.tothong.com
