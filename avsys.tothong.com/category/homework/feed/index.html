This new medium let us redefine our relationship with recorded sound and sonic environment. It offered a new way to listen and to play with sound. The app focuses on how sound can be a very powerful medium to evoke our awareness. Inspired by Eames’ The Power of Ten I seek to take that awareness of scale and sonify it into this app.
